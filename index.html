Day 1: Magic Kingdom

My wife and I took our 2-year-old son to Disney World for the very first time, and our adventure began at Magic Kingdom. We started with the PeopleMover, then raced around on the cars track ride, spun in circles at the Mad Tea Party teacups, and finished with the classic Dumbo ride.                 

Day 2: Animal Kingdom

On our second day, we visited Animal Kingdom, which felt like stepping into a wild adventure. Our son loved seeing the animals on the Kilimanjaro Safari, and we were amazed by the incredible details of the Tree of Life

.

Day 3: EPCOT

Day three took us to EPCOT, where we explored the World Showcase and enjoyed food from different countries. Our son was fascinated by the glowing lights on Spaceship Earth, and we all loved the calm ride inside the sphere. At night, the fireworks show over the lagoon was absolutely unforgettable.
